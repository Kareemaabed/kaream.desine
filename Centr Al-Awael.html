1- What is Al-Awael Center?

Answer: Al-Awael Center is an educational center that aims to provide all the materials for the preparatory and secondary levels in one place, characterized by special and distinctive characteristics with a group of the best teachers, in order to provide all means of comfort and success for students.

- What are the scientific and educational goals of the center?

the answer : Providing students with the basic skills of different sciences. - Formation of students' scientific thinking style and the ability to analyze information. - Dealing with the challenges of the current century, the first of which is openness to future sciences and their daily applications. 3 - Why is Al-Awael Center the most appropriate choice?

1- A distinguished place by providing a suitable educational environment. 2- The presence of a distinguished elite of the best teachers and those with extensive experience. 3- Periodic exams and comprehensive exams on an ongoing basis. 4- Ensuring to honor outstanding students to motivate students. 5- Ensuring the recreational side by doing some activities such as trips
